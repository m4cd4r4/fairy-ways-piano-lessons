# Musical Note Flashcards

*Practice recognizing notes with these flashcards!*

**How to Use:**
1. Print on cardstock
2. Cut out the cards
3. Quiz yourself: Look at the note on the staff, guess the letter!
4. Check by looking at the back
5. Practice until you can name them all instantly!

## Practice Games

### Game 1: Speed Round
- Shuffle the cards
- See how fast you can name all 7 notes
- Try to beat your time each day!

### Game 2: Fairy Match
- Say the fairy name instead of the letter
- "That's Rosabella!" instead of "That's C!"

### Game 3: Keyboard Find
- Look at a card
- Find and play the note on your piano
- No peeking at stickers!

### Game 4: Teach Roxy-Rose
- Pretend to teach your kitten the notes
- "See, Roxy-Rose? This one is E - that's Aisling!"
