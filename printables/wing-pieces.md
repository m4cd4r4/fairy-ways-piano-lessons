# Fairy Wing Pieces

*Collect all 12 pieces to complete your Fairy Music Guardian Wings!*

**How to Use:**
1. Print this page on cardstock
2. Cut out each wing piece as you earn it
3. Arrange them on the Wing Assembly Page
4. When all 12 are collected, your wings are complete!

---

## About the Wings

Your fairy wings will be a beautiful rainbow of colors - just like the seven fairy friends! Each wing piece represents a week of your journey and carries the magic of what you learned.

When you place all 12 pieces together, they form a complete pair of wings - proof that you are a true Fairy Music Guardian!
