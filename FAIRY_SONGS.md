## Song 1: Fairy Dust Falls

**Original Melody:** Hot Cross Buns

**Introduced:** Week 2, Lesson 3

**Level:** Beginner (3 notes)

### Notes to Play

Uses: E (purple) - D (yellow) - C (pink)

Right Hand Fingers: 3 - 2 - 1

```
Line 1:  E  -  D  -  C  (rest)
         3     2     1

Line 2:  E  -  D  -  C  (rest)
         3     2     1

Line 3:  E  -  E  -  E  -  E,   D  -  D  -  D  -  D
         3     3     3     3    2     2     2     2

Line 4:  E  -  D  -  C
         3     2     1
```

### Lyrics

```
Fair - y    dust   falls,    (rest)
Fair - y    dust   falls,    (rest)
Spar - kle  spar - kle,     twin - kle  twin - kle,
Mag - ic    fills  the      air!
```

### Alternative Verse (for variety!)

```
Ros - a  -  bel  - la,      (rest)
O  -  ri  - a   - na,       (rest)
Aish - ling  dreams  so,     pur - ple  star - light,
Fair - y     friends  are    here!
```

### Performance Tips

- Start SOFT, like fairy footsteps
- Get a tiny bit louder on the "sparkle, sparkle" part
- End soft again, like the magic settling
- Roxy-Rose loves this song played gently!

## Song 3: Síofra's Courage Song

**Original Melody:** Original (descending scale)

**Introduced:** Week 4, Lesson 8

**Level:** Beginner (5 notes)

### Notes to Play

Uses: G (black) - F (lime) - E (purple) - D (yellow) - C (pink)

Right Hand: Descending from pinky to thumb (5-4-3-2-1)

```
Line 1:  G  - G  - G  - G,
         5    5    5    5

Line 2:  F  - F  - F  - F,
         4    4    4    4

Line 3:  E  - E  - E  - E,
         3    3    3    3

Line 4:  D  - D  - D  - D,
         2    2    2    2

Line 5:  C!
         1
```

### Lyrics

```
I     am    brave,  I     am    strong,
I     can   do      it,
Yes   I     can,
GO!
```

### Alternative Words (for different feelings)

**When nervous:**
```
I     am    brave,  I     am    strong,
Sí  - o   - fra    is    with   me,
I     can   try,
NOW!
```

**When something is hard:**
```
Take  a     breath, try   a   - gain,
Ev  - 'ry  - one    learns  slow - ly,
I'll  get   there,
YES!
```

### Performance Tips

- Play this LOUD and PROUD - it's about COURAGE!
- Stand up tall while you play
- The final C (GO!) should be the strongest note
- Use this song when you need a boost of bravery!

## Song 4: The Rainbow Scale Song

**Original Melody:** Original (ascending/descending scale)

**Introduced:** Week 5, Lesson 10

**Level:** Beginner (5 notes)

### Notes to Play

Uses: C (pink) - D (yellow) - E (purple) - F (lime) - G (black) and back down

```
Going UP:    C  - D  - E  - F  - G
             1    2    3    4    5

Going DOWN:  G  - F  - E  - D  - C
             5    4    3    2    1
```

### Lyrics (Going Up)

```
Rain - bow   col - ors  shine!
```

### Lyrics (Going Down)

```
Mag - ic    all   a  - round!
```

### Full Version

```
Rain - bow   col - ors  shine! (going up)
Mag - ic    all   a  - round! (going down)
Fair - y    friends  I've  found! (going up)
Mu  - sic   ev  - 'ry - where! (going down)
```

### Performance Tips

- Keep a steady beat - like climbing stairs!
- Make "going up" sound happy and bright
- Make "going down" sound calm and settled
- Try getting louder going up, softer going down

## Song 5: Aria's Calm Song

**Original Melody:** Original (chord-based)

**Introduced:** Week 7, Lesson 14

**Level:** Intermediate (chord)

### Notes to Play

This is a CHORD - three notes played TOGETHER!

Left Hand plays: C (pink) + E (purple) + G (black) together
Fingers: 5 + 3 + 1

```
Play chord: C - E - G  (hold for 4 counts)
            5   3   1

Rest for 4 counts

Play chord again

Rest for 4 counts
```

### Lyrics (to speak or think while holding the chord)

```
(Play chord)  "Peace... and... calm... here..."
(Rest)        (breathe in slowly)
(Play chord)  "Safe... and... loved... always..."
(Rest)        (breathe out slowly)
```

### When to Use This Song

- When you feel worried or scared
- Before a big performance
- When Roxy-Rose needs calming
- During "fairy meditation" time
- Before bed

### Performance Tips

- Press all three keys at EXACTLY the same time
- Hold the sound and really LISTEN to it
- Let the vibrations calm your body
- This is Aria's gift to you!

## Tips for Singing While Playing

1. **Start with just the notes** - Learn the melody first
2. **Hum along** - Once you know the notes, hum the tune
3. **Add single words** - Say just the first word of each phrase
4. **Sing it all** - Put words and music together!
5. **Don't worry about mistakes** - The fairies love all music!

## Creating Your Own Fairy Songs

Use these fairy words in your own compositions:

**Fairy Names:** Rosabella, Oriana, Aisling, Fern, Síofra, Aria, Clover

**Magical Words:** sparkle, shimmer, rainbow, moonlight, starlight, dreams, wings

**Nature Words:** flowers, garden, forest, sky, clouds, rain, sunshine

**Feeling Words:** brave, calm, joyful, loving, curious, peaceful, magical

### Composition Starter

Try making a song using this pattern:
- Start on C (home)
- Go up to E (middle)
- Come back to C (home)
- Make up words that match!

Example:
```
C - D - E - D - C
"Rai - ning  down  fair - y   dust"
```
