## Guidelines

### Keeping the Magic

When contributing, please keep in mind:

- **Age Appropriateness**: This is for 4-6 year olds. Keep language simple and encouraging.
- **Positive Tone**: The fairies are always supportive! No scary elements.
- **Cultural Sensitivity**: The curriculum blends Irish Celtic and Australian elements. Be respectful of both cultures.
- **Musical Accuracy**: Ensure any musical content is correct for beginners.
